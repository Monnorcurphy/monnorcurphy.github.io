Books

Learning Web Design

Jennifer Niedesrst Robbins

A good introduction to HTML and CSS, a very rough introduction to Javascript. Perfect for beginners, who enjoy learning from textbook style books. Very detailed.

HTML and CSS

Jon Duckett

A fantastic introduction to HTML and CSS, with very colorful text, and basic descriptions of all the basics. Perfect for all beginners. The books presentation is easy to follow, easy to read, and helps teach about design, while also teaching you how to work with HTML and CSS. The drawback is, it is not as detailed as Learning Web Design, and you won't feel like reading Ms. Robbins book after you read this one.

Grokking Algorithims

Aditya Y. Bhargava

A very basic look at algorithims, with a fantastic intro to Big O notation, and some basic algorithims. What makes this book truly perfect for beginners is the number of things the author tells you to research. Once you finish the book, you know exactly what you will be reading and looking into next to improve your skills.

Cracking the Coding Interview

Gayle Laakmann McDowell
