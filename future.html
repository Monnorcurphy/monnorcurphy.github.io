Future Projects And Languages

Current Project

I am currently working on Wrighter, a Google Chrome extension, which naively checks emails (gmail specifically). Certain words it can correct easily (wurds, prapared), but others are more difficult (arunt). I will be increasing the number of edits a way a word can be for it to come up as a suggestion, I will also allow the users to see where their errors are, and select which word they meant. After the spell checker is within 90% accuracy, I will be moving onto a grammar checker, using Python's grammar checker. Once I have basic grammar funcionatily, I will be implementing more advanced linting, like the difference between imply and infer. Additionally, I would like to stop using Django as the backend, and switch over to node modules, so each user has all the information they need on their browser, rather than hitting our backend which could strangle the app when scaling up. I would also like to incorporate machine learning.
